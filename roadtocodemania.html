The IEEE Computer Society Student Branch Chapter of SLTC proudly presents “The Month of CodeGeniX 2021'' as the second initiative for the year 2021. The Month of CodeGeniX is a series of continuous programs and is the first of its kind in the history of SLTC The Month of CodeGeniX will span across both May and June 2021. The entire series of projects are designed with links interconnecting projects with each other. Thereby aiming to provide all computing and tech enthusiastic participants with the knowledge and guidance to step towards the IT industry and mark their ground. Furthermore, the series highly emphasizes empowering the SLTC student community by providing a unique experience paired with all essential guidance through an exciting yet educational series of programs. All projects are covered and assisted by well-known and highly qualified panelists and judges assuring the quality and quality of all programs.
